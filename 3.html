Keira Christin Knightley, is an English actress.

She is the recipient of an Empire Award. Pixie-ish British actress Keira Knightley went from a relative unknown to a blockbuster leading lady after 2002's sleeper soccer flick Bend It Like Beckham caught on with an international audience.

Born in Teddington, London, England, in 1985, young Knightley was enticed by the lure of cinema at an early age.

Playwright mother Sharman McDonald and actor father Will Knightley were at first reluctant to let their daughter follow them into show business.
